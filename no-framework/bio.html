Biography

Origins

Formed in Michigan in 2006, Salem pioneered the witch house genre.

The band's debut album, King Night, was released in 2010.

They are known for their dark, atmospheric sound and haunting visuals.

Members

  • John Holland - Vocals, Production
  • Heather Marlatt - Vocals, Synth
  • Jack Donoghue - Production

Each member brings a unique element to the band's sound, blending ethereal vocals with heavy beats and eerie synths.

Discography

  • King Night (2010)
  • I'm Still in the Night (2011)
  • Fires in Heaven (2020)

Their music has been influential in the underground electronic scene, and they continue to evolve their sound with each release.
